BUTTERFLY

Butterflies are insects belonging to the order Lepidoptera. They are known for their intricate wing patterns and graceful flight.



Facts


  • Butterflies have four life stages: egg, caterpillar (larva), chrysalis (pupa), and adult.
  • Their wings are covered in tiny scales, which give them their dazzling colors.
  • Butterflies taste with their feet!
